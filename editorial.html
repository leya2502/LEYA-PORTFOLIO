Desarrollo Zacatecas

The editorial project consists of the design and development of a catalog
that addresses the government of Zacatecas in a detailed and visually
attractive way. This catalog includes relevant information about the history,
culture, economy and politics of the region, presented in an organized and
accessible way for the reader. The design is characterized by its modern and
clean design, with a harmonious combination of text and images that capture
the essence and diversity of Zacatecas, making it an informative and
attractive tool for residents and
visitors interested in learning more about this important Mexican state.
The design of the catalog is characterized by its modernity and its focus on
usability, with a clean and attractive design that guides the reader through
the content in a fluid manner. Representative colors of Zacatecas and
graphic elements inspired by its culture and heritage are used, adding a
distinctive visual touch to the project.
