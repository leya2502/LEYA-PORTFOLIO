¡Hi!

Hello! I am Leya Velarde, a passionate graphic
designer with a creative vision and a passion for
transforming ideas into impactful visual realities.
With 4 years of experience in the design industry, I
have had the privilege of working on a wide range of
projects, from brand identities to editorial design,
illustration and web design.
My approach focuses on understanding the needs
and objectives of each project to create unique and
effective solutions that captivate the audience and
convey the desired message. I love experimenting
with different styles, techniques and tools, always
looking for new ways to innovate and exceed expectations.
My work process is based on close collaboration with
my clients, from the initial conceptualization phase to
the final delivery of the project, ensuring that every
step of the way reflects their vision and values.
Through my work, I aspire to inspire, excite and leave
a lasting impression in every project I undertake. I'm
excited to share my portfolio with you and explore
how we can work together to take your ideas to the next level.
Thank you for visiting my portfolio and I hope to have
the opportunity to collaborate with you soon!
